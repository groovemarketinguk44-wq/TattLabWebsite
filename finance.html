Questions

FAQ

Does applying for finance affect my credit score?
For 0% finance, we carry out a soft credit check during the application — this does not affect your credit score. A hard check is only performed if you proceed with a full credit agreement.
Is there a minimum spend for finance?
The 0% finance option is available on bookings of £200 and above. The Pay in 3 option via Klarna has no minimum spend. Deposit plans are available for all bookings.
What if I need to reschedule my appointment?
Finance plans are linked to your booking, not a specific date. If you need to reschedule, contact us at least 48 hours in advance and we'll move your appointment — your finance agreement stays in place.
Can I pay off my finance plan early?
Yes — early repayment is always welcome and there are no penalties for settling your plan ahead of schedule.
What ID do I need to apply?
You'll need to be 18+ and a UK resident. A valid form of photo ID (passport or driving licence) is required, along with proof of address dated within the last 3 months.
